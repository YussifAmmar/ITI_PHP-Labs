Travel Tips

• Always keep a copy of important documents like passport & ID.

• Learn basic phrases of the local language for smoother communication.

• Travel light and carry versatile clothing suitable for various weather conditions.

• Stay hydrated and take breaks to enjoy the journey, not just the destination.

• Use travel insurance for extra security and peace of mind.
